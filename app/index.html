Art Thief

Art Thief helps you review and manage the hundreds of artworks available at The Art League's Patrons’ Show in Alexandria, VA. Plus ,it makes the event and the planning for it more fun! With Art Thief, you'll be able to download this year’s artworks, rate them, and put them into a list based on your personal preference. Art Thief makes it easy to quickly keep track which artworks have been taken during the event, so that you're ready to make a selection when your name is called. Art Thief comes with bonus features that help you share the art on social media and vote for your favorite piece. Held annually on Presidents Day weekend for over 40 years, the Patrons' Show is a fabulous event where hundreds of artists donate their work, and patrons go home with a work of their own. The money raised supports The Art League, which provides fine art education and exhibiting opportunities in the DC metro area. Of course with hundreds of original artworks, creating and managing a long list of the artwork that interests you requires a lot of effort. Not to mention the scramble to keep your list up to date during the show! Art Thief is here to help, and it's free!
